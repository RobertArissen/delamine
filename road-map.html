Road Map

The Delamine office in Amersfoort is located in front of the railway station. Schiphol Airport has a regular, direct rail conection to Amersfoort. The office is easy to reach by car, parking garage avaible under the building.

1) Route from Amsterdam/Schiphol, Hilversum
Leave Motorway A1 at the first exit Amersfoort/Bunschoten. At the end of the exit turn right following the sign for Amersfoort/Hoogland. Continue on this road (Bunschoterstraat, changing into the Radiumweg), following the signs marked "P+R", to the Amsterdamseweg. At the traffic lights, turn left onto the Amsterdamseweg and keep following the "P+R" signs. When you come to a railway bridge, pass under it and take second road to the right; Utrechtseweg, continue on the Utrechtseweg and turn right at the Kon. Emmalaan (hospital on your left) leading you via the Kon. Wilhelminalaan to the BW laan car park on your left.

2) Route from Utrecht
Leave Motorway A28 at Exit Amersfoort Zuid/Maarn. At the end of the exit turn left, following the signs for Amersfoort/Isselt and keep going straight. Follow the route Amersfoort/Isselt/N221 under the road bridge and immediately turn left on the motorway going to Isselt/Soest/N221. At the roundabout Stichtse Rotonde, take the exit for Isselt, then immediately follow the sign for Amersfoort. Follow this road the Utrechtseweg, continue on the Utrechtseweg and turn approximately 25 meters before the railway to the left onto the Berkenweg. It is the third building on your right. You are at the back of the building.

3) Route from Hoevelaken junction
From Hoevelaken junction, keep following the A28 to Utrecht/Amersfoort. Take the exit Maarn/Amersfoort Zuid/Soest. Follow this road (N221) until you reach the roundabout (Stichtse Rotonde), then continue as for 2).

4) Route from Arnhem
Take the A12 toward Utrecht. Exit Maarsbergen/Leersum/Maarn (N226). At the end of the exit turn left following the signs for Maarsbergen/Amersfoort (N226). Keep following the signs for Amersfoort (N224) until you reach a crossing, turn right (N227) continue to follow the signs for Amersfoort, until you reach the A28, there you follow the sign Utrecht/Soest and take immediately the exit Maarn/Amersfoort Zuid. Follow this road (N221) until you reach the roundabout the Stichtse Rotonde, then continue as for 2).

5) Route from Rotterdam, the Hague and Den Bosch
Drive via Utrecht, then continue as for 2).
