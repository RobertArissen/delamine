Pulp and Paper

Even though digital technology is widespread, the paper-free office is still utopia. Delamine supplies important elements for the production of packaging cardboard and printer paper. For instance wet-strength resins and chelating agents which thicken the paper of make it ink- and/or waterproof. This is evident in labels and packaging but is also for transfer paper used in order blocks.
