Delamine concludes multimillion investment

Monday, 29 November 2010 11:10

"We clearly show and emphasize our commitment towards the market".

Delamine has finalized the expansion of her production capacity of Ethylene amines in Delfzijl, The Netherlands. The enterprise increased her name plate capacity up to 55.000 tons, a growth of 35%.

"This additional capacity enables us to consolidate our already strong position in the global Ethylene amines market." says general manager Gert van der Knaap. "This expansion has focused on increased capacity and flexibility with a special focus on the higher amines."

For this investment, Delamine has utilized self developed technology.

The global market shows a healthy growth and thanks to our expansion we will be able to grow along with the market.

Van der Knaap says: "In economical hard times, we have managed to expand our capacity. In doing so, Delamine has clearly emphasized her commitment towards the market. It enables our clients to do their business and Delamine to be better together with them. We are consolidating our reputation as a trustworthy business partner."
