Aerospace, Marine and Automotive

In the field of transport, our dynamics are very much versatile. Take our products out of the equation and literally nothing moves. We’re in gearshifts, in a wide range of synthetic materials, foam products, paints and varnishes. Even in the engine grease of boats, in aids for a more intensive combustion up to the light that flashes on the dashboard of your car. Worldwide, we’re the proud suppliers of many renowned car- boat- and aeroplane products. Adding value all along.
