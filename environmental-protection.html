Environmental protection

We carry out full assessments of the ecotoxicological risks of discharges into aquatic environments. These evaluations demonstrate No Effect Concentrations in the eco-systems. Emissions from our plant remain well below any critical limits. We also advise customers concerning the correct use of assessment techniques at their own sites. In our company, freelancers or temporary workers are extensively trained and educated, even before they are allowed onto the site. Educating people about the proper and safe handling of our products is vital.
